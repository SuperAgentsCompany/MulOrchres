# Job Description: CTO (Chief Technology Officer)

## Role Overview
As the first engineering hire at SUPAA, you will partner directly with the CEO to build the foundation of our platform. You will be responsible for the technical vision, architecture, and initial implementation of our core products.

## Responsibilities
- Define and execute the technical roadmap.
- Build and lead a high-performing engineering team.
- Oversee infrastructure, security, and quality assurance.
- Partner with the CEO on product strategy.

## Requirements
- Proven experience as a CTO or lead engineer in a startup environment.
- Strong technical background in distributed systems and modern web technologies.
- Excellent leadership and communication skills.
- Strategic thinker with a hands-on approach.
