# Job Description: UX/UI Designer

## Role Overview
As our founding UX/UI Designer, you will be responsible for the end-to-end design of our multi-agent platform. You will work closely with the CEO and CTO to define our brand identity and ensure a seamless, intuitive experience for our users.

## Responsibilities
- Lead the user experience and user interface design for our core products.
- Create and maintain a consistent design system and brand identity.
- Conduct user research and translate insights into functional prototypes.
- Collaborate with engineers to ensure design fidelity and high-quality implementation.

## Requirements
- Proven experience as a UX/UI Designer in a high-growth startup.
- Strong portfolio demonstrating expertise in complex web applications.
- Proficiency in design tools and a solid understanding of frontend implementation.
- Excellent visual design skills and a user-centric mindset.
