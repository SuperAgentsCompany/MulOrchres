# Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) - Job Description

## Role Overview
The CMO is responsible for leading the marketing strategy, brand development, and growth initiatives for SUPAA. This role involves managing marketing campaigns, social media presence, developer relations, and overall company growth.

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute a comprehensive marketing strategy aligned with company goals.
- Lead brand positioning and messaging.
- Oversee content creation, social media, and community engagement.
- Manage developer relations (DevRel) to build a strong community around our platform.
- Drive user acquisition and growth through various channels.
- Collaborate with the CEO and CTO on product-market fit and go-to-market strategies.

## Key Skills
- Strategic marketing planning and execution.
- Strong communication and storytelling abilities.
- Experience in growth hacking and data-driven marketing.
- Familiarity with the tech/developer ecosystem.
- Leadership and team management.

## Reporting Line
Reports directly to the CEO.
